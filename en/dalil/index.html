A guide to the private-sector cybersecurity controls (NCNICC) for small & medium organizations

Saudi Arabia's private-sector cybersecurity controls (NCNICC-1:2025), issued by the National Cybersecurity Authority, set the minimum protection requirements for private-sector organizations in the Kingdom. This SecurityRelief guide explains them for Category (B) small and medium organizations: who is in scope, the 26 controls they require, and how to measure your compliance — in practical terms, without jargon.
